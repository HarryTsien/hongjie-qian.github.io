Bioinformatics Lecture 1

Fundamental Concepts of Bioinformatics

Central Dogma: DNA, RNA and proteins

HGP: Human Genome Project 人类基因组计划

-omics: 组学

Bioinformatics:

  • Collection, processing & storage: computer scientist
  • Analysis & interpretation: biologist

➡Bioinformatics is a combination of

  (1) mathematical sciences

  (2) computer sciences

  (3) life sciences

Main research area of bioinformatics:

  • Genome sequence assembly
  • Gene identification
  • Gene function prediction
  • Genetic polymorphism analysis
  • Gene evolution
  • mRNA structure prediction
  • Gene chip design
  • Gene chip data analysis
  • Disease related gene analysis
  • Protein sequence analysis
  • Protein family classification
  • Protein structure prediction
  • Study of protein folding
  • Metabolic pathway analysis
  • Transcriptional regulation mechanism
  • Protein chip design
  • Protein microarray data analysis
  • Drug design (Computer-Assisted Drug Design, CADD)
  • …

Bioinformatics database:

  • Genomic database: EMBL, GenBank, DDBJ

  • Protein sequence database: SWISS-PROT, PIR

  • Protein sequence database: PDB

Three major gene database:

GenBank, EMBL Data Library, DDBJ (DNA Data Bank of Japan) ➡ Public free Available via Internet

KEGG: a collection of databases dealing with genomes, biological pathways, diseases, drugs, and chemical substances. （英语：(Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes，中文：京都基因与基因组百科全书，日语：京都遺伝子ゲノム百科事典）(link)

Scientific journals: Nucleic Acid Research
